Here is the recipe for lasagna:

Makes 6 servings

  • Ingredients
  • 1 pkg. oven ready lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb. sweet Italian sausage (browned)
  • 2 lb. shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 32 oz. part skim ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 lb. brown mushrooms (sliced, optional)
  • 1 cup water
